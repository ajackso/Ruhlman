Welcome to the Interact Section! Click on one of the small cards to reveal interesting statistics about Ruhlman Conferences at Wellesley College.
How many Ruhlman presentations have occurred in the past 20 years?
In the past 20 years of Ruhlman, there have been 3015 unique presentations.
Over the past 20 years of Ruhlman Conferences, what department has advised the most Ruhlman presentations?
In the past 20 years of Ruhlman, the Biological Sciences department has advised 323 Ruhlman presentations. Analysis provided by Nina-Marie Amadeo
Which Ruhlman Conference year hosted the most Ruhlman presentations to date?
The Ruhlman Conference in the year 2000 successfully hosted 187 student presentations.
Which Ruhlman Conference year hosted the least amount of Ruhlman presentations?
The 1997 Ruhlman Conference only hosted 77 student presentations, however, this was the first Ruhlman Conference in Wellesley history.
How many unique students have participated in the past 20 years of Ruhlman?
In the past 20 years of Ruhlman, 2650 students have participated in a Ruhlman Conference.
What department has advised the second most amount of Ruhlman presentations?
The Chemistry department has advised 320 Ruhlman presentations. Analysis provided by Nina-Marie Amadeo
Which advisor has advised the most Ruhlman presentations in the past 20 years of Ruhlman Conferences?
Professor Mary Allen of the Biological Sciences department has advised 60 individual Ruhlman presentations.Analysis provided by Maja Svanberg
What student major has been the most common major of Ruhlman presenters over the past 20 years of Ruhlman?
Pychology student majors have been the most popular student major in all 20 years of Ruhlman Conferences. 156 Pyschology majors have presented at Ruhlman thus far. Analysis provided by Jackie Hom
Which departments have advised the least amount of Ruhlman presentations?
The Quantitative Reasoning, Urban Studies, Medival/Renaissance and Media Arts and Sciences departments have only advised 1 Ruhlman presentation in the history of Ruhlman Conferences. Analysis provided by Nina-Marie Amadeo
Which advisor has advised the second most amount of Ruhlman presentations in the past 20 years of Ruhlman Conferences?
Professor David Haines of the Chemistry department and Professor Thomas Cushman of the Sociology department have each advised 53 Ruhlman presentations.
What student class year has consecutively dominated Ruhlman Conference presentations for the past 20 years?
Seniors have consistently presented the most at Ruhlman Conferences. For most conference years, they've made up at least 50% of student presenters. Analysis provided by Clara Sorensen and Anne Schwartz
Which Ruhlman Conference had no first-year student presenters?
The Ruhlman Conference of 2002 had no first-year students participate in the Ruhlman Conference. Analysis provided by Clara Sorensen and Anne Schwartz
Which advisors advised the most Ruhlman presentations in 2008?
Professors Rosanna Hertz of the Women's and Gender Studies dept, Mary Allen of the Biological Sciences dept, and Irene Mata of the Women's and Gender Studies dept each advised 7 students during the 2008 Ruhlman Conference. Analysis provided by Anne Schwartz
What is the third most common student major of Ruhlman presenters over the past 20 years of Ruhlman?
Economics student majors have been the third most popular student major in all 20 years of Ruhlman Conferences. 98 English majors have presented at Ruhlman. Analysis provided by Julia Chmyz
What department has advised the most students outside of it's own department?
Over the past 20 years of Ruhlman, 93 Ruhlman presentations have been advised by Biology advisors and presented by non-Biology student majors. Analysis provided by Julia Chmyz
How many Ruhlman student presenters had an advisor from from a department different than their student major?
1659 or about 55% of Ruhlman presentations have presentation advisors and students from different majors/departments. Analysis provided by Valerie Zhao
What student major has been advised outside of it's own major department the most?
59 Neuroscience student majors have been advised by non-Neuroscience advisors. Analysis provided by Julia Chmyz
What student major has been the second most common major of Ruhlman presenters over the past 20 years of Ruhlman?
English student majors have been the second most popular student major. 111 English majors have presented at Ruhlman. Analysis provided by Julia Chmyz
